Shadow Tag

A game of tag. You are the red player. If you're outlined in yellow, then you're it. Catch the blue player to tag them. Otherwise, avoid being caught by the other player! You are briefly frozen when caught.

When not it, collect the green treasures to get points. You cannot see behind obstacles.

The computer-controlled blue player was trained using reinforcement learning.

Key Action
W forward
S backward
A turn left
D turn right
